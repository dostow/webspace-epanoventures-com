ENVIRONMENTAL CONSULTING
Richmond York provides environmental advisory services to the private, public and non-profit sectors. Specifically,

a) Environmental Impact Assessments
Environmental Risk Assessments, Impact Mitigation Monitoring, Environmental Evaluation Studies for sectors spanning Oil & Gas, Telecommunications, Construction, Manufacturing, etc

b) Renewables / Solar
Business development for solar technologies – solar energy systems and solar powered products – for commercial, household and municipal use.

c) Agro-forestry
Business support towards the commercialisation of industrial wood products from West African plantations – for export (as biomass fuel in Europe), for power generation, and for local market.

d) General Advisory
Climate Change policy-related initiatives; Urban Development, etc
